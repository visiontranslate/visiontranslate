About Me

Born and living in Auckland, New Zealand, Nick has a passion for both web design and Chinese to English translation. Nick studied Chinese at University level in both Auckland and Taiwan and is a member of the New Zealand Society of Translators and Interpreters (NZSTI). Nick's accumulated years of translating experience working for agencies, in-house translation departments, and as a freelancer in both Taiwan and New Zealand. Nick's equally passionate about web design - he has a Diploma in Web Design and Development, Computer Science degree, years of work experience in the IT industry and endeavours to keep up-to-date with the latest web design and development trends.
